About Pearlcon LLC

Saudi-based industrial engineering & services provider

Pearlcon LLC Saudi Arabia is a Saudi-registered engineering services company focused on field execution and technical support. Operating independently within Saudi Arabia, we support critical industrial assets with experienced site teams, strong safety culture, and dependable service delivery.

Pearlcon LLC Saudi Arabia is part of Pearlcon Business Services Ltd, United Kingdom , a UK-based procurement, engineering supply, and strategic sourcing company with over 15 years of continuous operation and growth. Pearlcon Business Services specializes in the supply, installation, and commissioning of MRO products, industrial equipment, and engineering solutions across sectors including Rail, Retail, Manufacturing, Oil & Gas, Power & Energy, Water & Wastewater, and Construction.

Services

Our Core Engineering & Industrial Services

Electrical Transmission & Automation

Electrical installation, testing and commissioning services including power distribution systems, protection relays, control panels and industrial automation. We provide preventive and corrective maintenance, fault diagnosis and troubleshooting support for critical operations.

Mechanical & Industrial Services

Mechanical installation and maintenance services covering static and rotating equipment, industrial piping and mechanical works. We support shutdowns, turnarounds and planned maintenance activities across industrial facilities.

Industrial Refrigeration & HVAC

Design, installation, testing and commissioning of industrial and commercial HVAC systems including chillers, AHUs, ventilation and cooling systems. Comprehensive preventive and corrective maintenance solutions are provided.

Steam, Gas & Piping Systems

Installation and maintenance of steam and condensate piping systems, industrial gas pipework and fittings. Services include pressure testing, inspections, certification, repairs, modifications and system upgrades.

Oil & Gas Plant Services

On-site oil and gas plant support including equipment inspection, servicing, maintenance, modifications and upgrades. We assist clients in meeting operational safety, compliance and performance requirements.

Railway & Infrastructure Services

Electrical and mechanical support services for railway and infrastructure projects, including maintenance, asset support and technical service solutions for reliable and safe operations.
